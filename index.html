Welcome to the Gilligin's Island Repository!

To add this repository, follow the directions for your distribution bellow.

For Ubuntu: Paste the commands bellow one at a time into your favorite terminal.

wget -qO - http://apt.broochian.com/gilliginsisland.gpg.key | sudo apt-key add -
echo 'deb http://apt.broochian.com ubuntu main' | sudo tee /etc/apt/sources.list.d/gilliginsisland.list
sudo apt update

For iOS: Open this page on your jailbroken iOS device and tap this link.
